## Strategy for Scoring Sources
- **A = Official/State Publications**: High reliability, low bias. Direct facts on rules, passing scores, dates, and official blueprints.
- **B = Direct Candidates' Recalls (with consensus)**: Medium-high reliability for exam topics. When multiple independent comments on Facebook/Pantip confirm the same question/answer or topic (e.g. "อนุกรมข้อนี้ตอบ 125", "ตารางออกเรื่องประชากร").
- **C = Tutoring Centers/Academic Blogs**: Medium reliability. Good for structured lists and tips, but could have promotional intent or recycled old questions.
- **D = Isolated Opinions/Unverified Claims**: Low reliability. Single comments on social media with no verification or contradictory posts.
